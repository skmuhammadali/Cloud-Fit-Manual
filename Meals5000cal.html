5000 Calorie Meal Plans – Veg & Non-Veg

🥦 Vegetarian Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast (~1000 kcal)

  • 2 cups oats with whole milk, nuts & banana
  • Peanut butter toast (2 slices)
  • 1 boiled sweet potato
  • Protein shake with almond milk

🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (~800 kcal)

  • Fruit smoothie (banana, peanut butter, oats, yogurt)
  • Granola bar
  • Handful of almonds and walnuts

🍛 Lunch (~1000 kcal)

  • 1.5 cups brown rice
  • 1 cup rajma or chole curry
  • 2 chapatis with ghee
  • Paneer curry (150g)
  • Salad with olive oil dressing + curd

🌇 Evening Snack (~800 kcal)

  • Peanut butter banana smoothie
  • Trail mix (nuts, raisins, seeds)
  • 1 energy bar

🌙 Dinner (~1000 kcal)

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • Tofu/paneer curry (150g)
  • Vegetable sabzi (1.5 cups)
  • 2 chapatis with ghee
  • Soup or dal + sautéed greens

🌜 Bedtime Snack (~400 kcal)

  • Warm milk with honey
  • Dates or banana
  • Handful of mixed nuts

🍗 Non-Vegetarian Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast (~1000 kcal)

  • 4 egg omelette with cheese & vegetables
  • 2 whole wheat toast with butter or avocado
  • Banana or apple
  • Milk or protein shake

🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (~800 kcal)

  • Chicken sandwich with cheese
  • Smoothie (banana, oats, peanut butter, yogurt)
  • Handful of nuts and seeds

🍛 Lunch (~1000 kcal)

  • 1.5 cups rice
  • 200g chicken or fish curry
  • 1 chapati with ghee
  • Vegetable sabzi
  • Salad + curd or raita

🌇 Evening Snack (~800 kcal)

  • Grilled chicken wrap or boiled eggs
  • Fruit smoothie or milkshake
  • Peanuts or dry fruit bar

🌙 Dinner (~1000 kcal)

  • 200g grilled chicken/fish
  • 1 cup brown rice or quinoa
  • 1–2 phulkas
  • Soup or lentils + steamed veggies
  • Greek yogurt or curd

🌜 Bedtime Snack (~400 kcal)

  • Warm milk with honey
  • 1 fruit or 2 dates
  • Boiled egg or peanut butter toast
