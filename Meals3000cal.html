3000 Calorie Meal Plans – Veg & Non-Veg

🥦 Vegetarian Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast (~700 kcal)

  • 2 cups cooked oats with milk
  • 1 banana + 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp flaxseeds
  • 10 almonds & 4 walnuts

🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (~400 kcal)

  • 1 fruit smoothie with yogurt + honey
  • Granola (1/2 cup)

🍛 Lunch (~750 kcal)

  • 1.5 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup paneer curry
  • 1 small chapati
  • Salad + 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 cup curd

🌇 Evening Snack (~300 kcal)

  • Roasted chickpeas or sprouts
  • 1 glass buttermilk or lassi

🌙 Dinner (~650 kcal)

  • 1 cup quinoa + 1 bowl dal
  • 1 mixed vegetable curry
  • 1 phulka
  • Steamed broccoli/carrots

🌜 Bedtime Snack (~200 kcal)

  • Warm milk with honey
  • 2 dates or a small banana

🍗 Non-Vegetarian Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast (~700 kcal)

  • 3 boiled eggs or 2 egg omelette with veggies
  • 2 whole wheat toast + peanut butter
  • Banana or apple
  • 1 glass milk or coffee

🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (~400 kcal)

  • Fruit smoothie with protein powder or yogurt
  • Handful of mixed nuts

🍛 Lunch (~750 kcal)

  • 1 cup cooked rice + 150g grilled chicken curry
  • Vegetable stir-fry
  • 1 chapati
  • 1 bowl curd or raita

🌇 Evening Snack (~300 kcal)

  • Boiled egg + roasted peanuts
  • 1 cup black tea or coffee

🌙 Dinner (~650 kcal)

  • 100–150g grilled fish or chicken
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • Vegetables (steamed or sautéed)
  • Soup or salad

🌜 Bedtime Snack (~200 kcal)

  • Milk + 2 dates or a protein bar
