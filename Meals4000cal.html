4000 Calorie Meal Plans – Veg & Non-Veg

🥦 Vegetarian Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast (~900 kcal)

  • 2 cups cooked oats with whole milk
  • 1 banana + 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 10 almonds + 5 walnuts
  • 1 boiled potato or sweet potato

🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (~600 kcal)

  • Smoothie with banana, milk, protein powder, peanut butter, oats
  • Granola bar or energy bites

🍛 Lunch (~900 kcal)

  • 1.5 cups cooked rice or millets
  • 1.5 cups rajma or chole
  • 1 chapati with ghee
  • 1 bowl curd + salad with olive oil
  • Paneer cubes (100g)

🌇 Evening Snack (~600 kcal)

  • Peanut butter toast (2 slices)
  • Dry fruits + lassi or banana shake

🌙 Dinner (~800 kcal)

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • Vegetable korma or curry with tofu/paneer
  • 2 phulkas with ghee
  • Soup or dal + sautéed greens

🌜 Bedtime Snack (~200 kcal)

  • Warm milk with honey
  • 1 banana or 2 dates

🍗 Non-Vegetarian Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast (~900 kcal)

  • 3 egg omelette with veggies & cheese
  • 2 whole wheat toast with butter
  • Banana or avocado
  • Milk or protein shake

🕙 Mid-Morning Snack (~600 kcal)

  • Chicken sandwich or grilled egg sandwich
  • Fruit smoothie with nuts

🍛 Lunch (~900 kcal)

  • 1.5 cups rice + 150–200g chicken curry or fish fry
  • 1 chapati
  • Salad with olive oil
  • Curd or raita

🌇 Evening Snack (~600 kcal)

  • Boiled egg + peanuts
  • 1 banana or date shake
  • Protein bar

🌙 Dinner (~800 kcal)

  • Grilled fish or chicken breast (200g)
  • Brown rice or quinoa
  • Mixed sautéed veggies
  • 1 chapati + soup

🌜 Bedtime Snack (~200 kcal)

  • Warm milk + 2 dates
  • 1 slice toast or small fruit
